VendorConnect was started to bridge the gap between local vendors and digital tools. Many small business owners in Hassan and Karnataka have great products but little online presence. We want to change that — one shop at a time — with a platform that is simple, local, and truly helpful.

Frequently Asked Questions
Who can use VendorConnect?
Any local vendor or small business owner in Hassan and surrounding areas. This includes kirana stores, vegetable sellers, flower vendors, tailors, repair shops, dairy sellers, pharmacies, and more.
Is it really free to join?
Yes! Registering your shop and listing your products is completely free. There are no hidden charges for getting your shop online.
Do I need any technical skills or a computer?
No technical skills needed at all. If you can use WhatsApp on a phone, you can use VendorConnect. We also provide a step-by-step guide on the registration page to help you get started easily.
How long does it take for my shop to go live?
After you register, our admin team reviews your shop within 24 hours and approves it. You'll be notified via your phone number once your shop is live.
How do I log in after registering?
Go to the "Vendor Login" page (from the navigation at the top). Enter the username, shop name, and password you created when you registered. If you forget your password, contact us and we'll help.
What if I need help using the website?
You can send us a message through the Contact page, email us at [email protected], or call us on +91 9876543210. Our team is happy to guide you personally, even in Kannada.
Can customers contact me directly?
Yes! Your phone number is shown on your shop page so customers can call or WhatsApp you directly. There is no intermediary and no commission charged.
