How to Register
Your Shop

Follow these simple steps — no computer skills needed. If you need help, call us at +91 9876543210.

1
Choose a Username Pick a simple name — like your name or shop name. Example: "raju_store" or "divya_flowers". Use only letters and numbers, no spaces.
2
Enter Your Shop Name Write your shop's full name as you'd like customers to see it. Example: "Raju's Fresh Vegetables".
3
Choose a Business Type Select the category that best describes your shop from the dropdown list.
4
Add Your Phone Number This is how customers will contact you. Enter your WhatsApp number if possible.
5
Create a Password Choose a password you'll remember easily — at least 6 characters. Write it down somewhere safe. You'll use this to log in every time.
6
Click Register & Wait Press the green button. The admin will review and approve your shop within 24 hours. You'll receive a confirmation call or message.
